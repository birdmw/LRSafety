Turn-Around Checklist for Climbers (0-N point system, total score 0-10):

    Climbing Experience (0-3 points): Consider your experience in similar climbing conditions (0 - experienced, 2 - some experience, 3 - no experience).
    Climbing Partner(s) (0-3 points): Assess the presence and experience of your climbing partner(s) (0 - experienced partner(s), 2 - some experience, 3 - solo or inexperienced partner(s)).
    Mental State (0-2 points): Consider your mental state and focus (0 - focused and confident, 1 - somewhat focused, 2 - unfocused or overly anxious).

3-5 points: Consider turning around or altering your plan based on the specific concerns identified.
6-8 points: Strongly consider turning around due to multiple concerns or significant risks.
9-10 points: Turn around immediately, as the risks outweigh the potential benefits of continuing the climb.
